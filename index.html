Installation

Installing Plug X couldn't be any simpler. Just click on the download button up top or visit the Chrome Store page to download.

Tips & Tricks

Plug X has commands built in that let you access a plethora of features. To view the list of commands, type:

/commands

Automuting songs is an unique feature of Plug X, but is only accessible through commands at the moment.

To automute the current song, type:

/automute

To remove the current song from your automuted list, type:

/removemute

A quick way to check which version of the extension you have is with the command:

/version

Video/Audio stream can quickly be turned on or off with a simple command:

/stream on
/stream off
